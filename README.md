## About

I am a **Senior AI / Machine Learning Engineer** and **AI researcher** with 5+ years of experience designing, training, evaluating, and documenting machine learning systems for healthcare, education, and applied industrial AI.

My work focuses on building reliable AI pipelines for **medical imaging**, **computer vision**, **clinical risk prediction**, and **AI-driven learning analytics**. I have worked on deep learning systems for hip-fracture risk prediction, IVF outcome prediction, burn-image analysis, and student-performance prediction.

Alongside engineering and research, I am the **Founder & Lead Instructor of CS50x Tehran** and **CS50x Rasht**, where I teach computer science, Python, algorithms, and AI foundations to thousands of learners.
